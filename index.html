About

I make Electronic Dance Music (EDM) and its sub-genres like Future Bounce, Future House, Future Bass, Melodic Dubstep etc. I have been a keen EDM listener since I was a kid and I was always interested in making my own tracks like many EDM artists like Martin Garrix, David Guetta, Marshmello, Skrillex, Alan Walker, Zedd etc. I started experimenting in making music with my favourite app on mobile back then, Music Maker JAM and make countless tracks. But it wasn't that skillful because it was only a loop playing on my control. When I was 16, I got a new PC and since then I use FL Studio 21 as my DAW.

Also, I have a huge interest in programming and I am a self-taught programmer. I have been coding since class 8th and have been making many projects since then. I have made many websites and apps as a hobby and I am looking forward to make more in the future.
